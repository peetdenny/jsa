The world's tiniest space agency starts with the following bits:


Part What It Does
Rocket the rocket is the bit that does all the exploring
Motor the part that the flames come out of
Ground Station the part that the rocket launches off
Rod holds the rocket straight up so it flies to the clouds (instead of into the ground)
Wadding keeps the parachute from burning when the nose cone is blown off
Igniter ignites the motor so the rocket can fly
Ignition System sends an electric charge via wires to the igniter to start the rocket
