Founders - Main Branch

  • Indigo Denny: Chief Rocketry Officer & Insect Enthusiast

  • "I started JSA because I like doing electronics, and I'd seen other people building model planes, and I think that rockets are unique. My dad also is good at programming and he introduced me to rockets and they're really fun. I like stuff to do with exploration like space and the deep sea. I think that one day we'll launch a weather ballon that should go up to space, at least for a little bit. I have a few hobbies, but I think this is the one that suits me the best!"
